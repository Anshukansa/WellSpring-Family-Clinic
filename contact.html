Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us at (03) 1234 5678 or using our online appointment form. We'll confirm your booking within 24 hours.

Do you accept walk-in patients?

We primarily operate by appointment to ensure quality care. However, we do accept urgent walk-ins based on availability.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your Medicare card, any referrals, current medications list, and relevant medical history.

Do you bulk bill?

We offer bulk billing for eligible patients. Please contact us to discuss payment options and eligibility.

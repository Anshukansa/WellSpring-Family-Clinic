About WellSpring Family Clinic

Dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services to our community

Our Story

WellSpring Family Clinic is a newly established community health center dedicated to providing accessible, quality healthcare to local families. We founded our clinic with a vision to create a welcoming environment where families can receive comprehensive medical care.

As a community-focused health center, we prioritize building strong relationships with our patients and providing personalized care that meets the unique needs of each family we serve.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, family-centered healthcare services that improve the health and wellbeing of our local community. We strive to deliver compassionate care in a welcoming environment while building lasting relationships with the families we serve.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every patient with empathy and understanding
  • Excellence: We maintain the highest standards of medical care
  • Integrity: We practice with honesty and transparency
  • Respect: We value the dignity of every individual
  • Community: We are committed to serving our local families

Meet Our Medical Team

Dr. Sarah Mitchell

General Practitioner & Clinic Director

MBBS, FRACGP

Dr. Mitchell has over 15 years of experience in family medicine. She specializes in women's health, chronic disease management, and preventive care. She is passionate about building long-term relationships with her patients.

Dr. James Wong

General Practitioner

MBBS, FRACGP, Dip. Child Health

Dr. Wong brings 12 years of experience with a special interest in pediatrics and family health. He is known for his gentle approach with children and comprehensive care for families.

Dr. Emma Taylor

General Practitioner

MBBS, FRACGP, Cert. Skin Cancer Medicine

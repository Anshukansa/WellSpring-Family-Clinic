Our Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare services tailored to meet your needs

General Practice

Our general practice services provide comprehensive primary healthcare for patients of all ages. We focus on preventive care, early diagnosis, and effective treatment of a wide range of medical conditions.

What We Offer:

  • Routine health check-ups and screenings
  • Diagnosis and treatment of acute and chronic conditions
  • Preventive care and health education
  • Management of diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease
  • Minor surgical procedures
  • Health assessments and medical certificates

Immunizations

Stay protected with our comprehensive immunization services. We provide vaccines for all ages, from childhood immunizations to adult boosters and travel vaccines.

Vaccination Services:

  • Childhood immunization schedule
  • Adult vaccination programs
  • Travel vaccines and consultations
  • Influenza vaccines (seasonal)
  • COVID-19 vaccinations
  • Occupational health vaccines

Family Health

Comprehensive healthcare services designed for the whole family. We provide specialized care for women, men, children, and elderly patients with a focus on family-centered health.

Family Health Services:

  • Women's health including cervical screening and family planning
  • Men's health assessments and prostate screening
  • Pediatric care and child development monitoring
  • Elderly care and chronic disease management
  • Mental health support and counseling
  • Family planning and pregnancy care

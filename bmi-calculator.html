Understanding BMI Categories

Underweight

BMI less than 18.5

You may need to gain weight. Consult with our healthcare professionals for guidance.

Normal Weight

BMI 18.5 - 24.9

You have a healthy weight. Maintain it with regular exercise and balanced nutrition.

Overweight

BMI 25.0 - 29.9

You may benefit from weight management. Consider lifestyle changes and professional advice.

Obese

BMI 30.0 and above

Health risks may be increased. We recommend consulting with our healthcare team.

Important Note

BMI is a useful screening tool, but it doesn't directly measure body fat or individual health status. Factors like muscle mass, bone density, and overall body composition aren't considered. For a comprehensive health assessment, please book an appointment with our medical professionals.
